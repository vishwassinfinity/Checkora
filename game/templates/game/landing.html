Privacy Policy

1

Information We Collect

Checkora only collects basic information necessary to manage user authentication and orchestrate your chess matches:

  • Account Data: Username, email address, and encrypted password profiles when you register.
  • Gameplay Metrics: Move history, win/loss stats, match durations, and time preferences for leaderboard rankings.
  • Technical Logs: Minimal session tokens to keep you securely logged in across page reloads.
2

How Your Data is Used

Your data remains dedicated exclusively to powering your active application experiences:

  • Maintain your personalized player profile dashboard and performance metrics.
  • Calculate real-time material scores and process live move-validation checks.
  • Optimize the sync pipeline between backend systems and computation services.
3

Cookies and Session Storage

We use essential cookies and local storage tokens to manage authentication states and secure form submissions against CSRF. No tracking cookies or behavioral profile monitors are embedded inside the platform.

4

Data Retention & Security

We retain your profile credentials and match logs for as long as your account remains active. Passwords are securely stored using Django authentication hashing mechanisms.

5

Open Source Transparency & User Rights

In alignment with our open-source framework and GSSoC community policies, users maintain full authority over their data and may request account deletion or data removal at any time.

6

Contact & Community Channels

If you have questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the project maintainers directly.
